# SaaS Ideas — Traffic First Strategy

> Selected: 2026-02-17
> Goal: Maximize traffic first, monetize later

---

## Chosen Ideas

### 1. X vs Y — Universal Comparison Engine
- **Concept:** AI-powered comparison platform for anything (tech, cities, careers, products, games, food, etc.)
- **Why:** Every pair of popular things = an auto-generated page. 500K+ unique pages via programmatic SEO.
- **Search volume:** "X vs Y" queries total millions/month across all categories
- **Key differentiator:** No dedicated platform owns this space. Current results are random blog posts.
- **Traffic target:** 5-10M/month within 12 months
- **Features:**
  - AI pulls data from multiple sources → structured, unbiased comparison
  - User voting on winners → community engagement → fresh content
  - Categories: tech, cities, universities, careers, games, food, cars, languages
  - Programmatic SEO: 1,000 items × 1,000 = 500K+ unique pages

### 2. AI Dream Interpreter
- **Concept:** Clean, AI-powered dream interpretation platform replacing garbage ad-farm sites from 2005
- **Why:** Massive long-tail keywords (thousands of dream symbols), deeply personal = high sharing
- **Search volume:**
  - "Dream about snakes" — 200K/mo
  - "Dream about falling" — 150K/mo
  - "Dream about teeth falling out" — 100K/mo
  - Thousands more dream symbol combinations
- **Key differentiator:** Current dream sites are terrible UX with popup hell. Modern AI + clean UI wins instantly.
- **Traffic target:** 3-5M/month within 12 months (top dream site gets 15M/month with bad product)
- **Features:**
  - Describe dream → AI gives personalized, detailed interpretation
  - Auto-generate 5,000+ pages: "Dream about [symbol] meaning"
  - Dream journal for retention
  - Social sharing of interesting dreams

### 3. Dupe — Find the Cheap Version of Anything Expensive
- **Concept:** The definitive dupe database. Search any expensive product → see ranked affordable alternatives with match scores, real reviews, real photos, and buy links.
- **Why:** "Dupe" culture has exploded (TikTok #dupe = billions of views) but NO dedicated website exists. The entire ecosystem lives in scattered TikTok videos and blog posts.
- **Search volume:**
  - "Lululemon dupe" — 500K+/mo
  - "Dyson Airwrap dupe" — 300K+/mo
  - "Stanley cup dupe" — 300K+/mo
  - "[expensive brand] dupe" combined — millions/mo and growing
- **Key differentiator:** No dedicated dupe website exists anywhere. First mover wins.
- **AI-proof because:** Live prices, real product photos, real review counts, match scores from spec comparison, one-click affiliate buy links, price tracking alerts — none replicable by a chatbot.
- **Revenue from Day 1:** Every buy link is an Amazon affiliate link (3-8% commission)
- **Traffic targets:**
  - Month 1: 2,000+ products with dupes, 5,000+ SEO pages
  - Month 3: Price tracking + alerts
  - Month 6: 500K monthly visitors, $5-10K/month affiliate revenue
  - Month 12: 1M+ monthly visitors, $30-50K/month
- **Features:**
  - Search any expensive product → ranked dupes with match % score
  - AI compares specs/materials/reviews to calculate match score
  - Auto-scraped from Amazon, Target, Walmart, Shein
  - Categories: fashion, beauty, home, tech, kitchen, fitness
  - Side-by-side visual comparison
  - Price drop alerts
  - Programmatic SEO: "best [brand] dupes 2026", "[product] affordable alternative"
- **Roadmap:**
  - Week 1-2: MVP with top 200 most-searched expensive products
  - Month 1: Expand to 2,000+ products across all categories
  - Month 2: 5,000+ programmatic SEO pages
  - Month 3: Price tracking + alerts feature
